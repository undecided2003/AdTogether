# AdTogether Content Policy

The AdTogether platform requires all advertisements to comply with our content policy. Ads that violate these guidelines will be rejected during the submission process.

## 1. Prohibited Content

### Sexual Content
- Explicit sexual content, pornography, or mature sexual themes.
- Nudity or suggestive poses.
- Links to adult entertainment, adult dating sites, or adult merchandise.

### Violence and Gore
- Graphic or gratuitous violence, gore, or physical trauma.
- Promotion of violence against any person, group, or animal.
- Depictions of real-life tragedy, abuse, or torture.

### Drugs and Illegal Substances
- Promotion, sale, or use of illegal drugs, recreational drugs, or drug paraphernalia.
- Irresponsible use of regulated substances (e.g., alcohol, tobacco, vaping) targeted towards minors.

### Hate Speech and Harassment
- Content that promotes discrimination, hate, or violence against individuals or groups based on race, ethnicity, religion, sexual orientation, disability, or gender.
- Harassment, bullying, or intimidation.

### Illegal Acts and Regulated Goods
- Promotion of illegal acts or terrorism.
- Sale of weapons, firearms, ammunition, or explosives.

## 2. Enforcement
All ads submitted to the platform are subject to automated screening via AI (DeepSeek model integration) and regular manual audits. 
- **Automated Rejection**: Any ad found to violate this policy upon submission will be immediately rejected. The user will be notified of the exact reason (e.g. "Violates content guidelines for violence").
- **Account Action**: Repeated attempts to upload content violating these rules may result in the suspension or permanent banning of the advertiser's account.
